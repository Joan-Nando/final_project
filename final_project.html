My name is Joan Nando Pratama, and I was born in 2005 in the small town of Magelang. Growing up in a place rich with history and surrounded by natural beauty, I've always been inspired by the tranquility of my hometown. Magelang, known for its proximity to the magnificent Borobudur Temple, has shaped my perspective on life—balancing tradition with modern aspirations. Now, as I step into the future, I carry the values and lessons from my roots while exploring new opportunities and challenges that await me.

I enjoy mountain climbing, a hobby that allows me to connect with nature and challenge myself physically and mentally. The sense of accomplishment after reaching a summit is incredibly rewarding, and the peaceful, scenic views along the way make every climb worth it. Whether it’s exploring new trails or conquering familiar peaks, mountain climbing always offers a refreshing experience.
